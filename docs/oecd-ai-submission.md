## Tool Name
Pegasus — AI Compliance Validation Framework

## Short Description
Open-source framework for automated compliance validation of AI systems against international standards including ISO 42001, EU AI Act, NIST AI RMF, and OWASP guidelines.

## Long Description
Pegasus provides an open-source, evidence-based compliance evaluation framework for AI systems. Using a policy engine powered by OPA Rego (via the Rust-native regorus evaluator), it evaluates evidence collected from AI infrastructure against 60+ policies mapped to formal standards. The framework supports 12 certification profiles including ISO/IEC 42001 (AI Management Systems), the EU AI Act (high-risk systems), NIST AI RMF 1.0, and OWASP Top 10 for Large Language Models.

Key capabilities:
- Automated policy-based compliance evaluation
- Multi-standard certification scoring with pass thresholds
- Dual-agent cross-review (security + compliance) with confidence scoring
- Content-addressable evidence store for audit trails
- Extensible policy library (community contributions welcome)

## OECD AI Principles Alignment

- Robustness, security, and safety (adversarial resistance, guardrail testing)
- Accountability (evidence-based audit trails, compliance scoring)
- Human-centred values and fairness (bias detection, demographic parity policies)
